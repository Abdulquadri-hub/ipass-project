About Us

Our company, Information Processing And System Services Limited (IPASS) was established and registered as a limited liability company under Nigerian laws in 2016 to carry out work in the field of ICT, especially in education sector. We offer our services to clients in Nigeria and other West African sub region but we are poised to expand our operations to the rest of the African continent and beyond.

IPASS is a recognized innovative brand that provide solutions to assist Individuals, Organizations and Governments especially in providing much needed basic ICT (Information and Communication Technology) technologies, resources and skills in the area of education.

Ours is an ICT professional firm that provide IT support, helpdesk and consultancy services. We sell and support the full range of IT hardware and software and offer maintenance contracts up to 24x7 through our certified and experienced staff.

We pride ourselves on providing a world class service through our highly experienced and professional team. Together with our sister firm, we have built a long established reputation and forged strong lasting business relationships on the principles of trust and through our investment in people and ethical business practices.
